FAQ

Where is HackHive?

On campus at West Virginia State University (WVSU) in Institute, WV.

What’s the theme?

Securing Air Quality Data in Distributed Sensor Networks — protecting integrity, availability, and trust in environmental sensor data end-to-end.

What should I bring?

Laptop + charger, ID, and anything you need to build/demo. If you’re using sensors, bring your own hardware (unless organizers provide kits).

Do I need a team?

Teams are encouraged, but you can also join solo and form a team during the event (edit based on your official rules).
